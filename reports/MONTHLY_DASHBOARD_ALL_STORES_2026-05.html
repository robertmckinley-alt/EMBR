💡 Category & Consumer Trends — May 2026

📊 Product Category Trends

🌿Flower: Still #1 at ~40% of MA weekly revenue. Declining wholesale prices = margin pressure.
🔥Pre-rolls: +12% revenue growth (fastest growing category Jun 2023–Jun 2024). Infused pre-rolls dominate (43% of segment).
💨Vape: Stable ~21% of MA mix; declining in some markets from oversupply.
🍬Edibles: 13% share; consumer shift toward lower doses (2.5–10mg preferred). Opportunity for low-dose SKU expansion.
🥤Beverages: Fastest growing nationally (+79–112% in MI/OH). Early-stage opportunity to stock ahead of curve.

👥 Consumer Demographics

📱Millennials (born 1981–96): 44.6% of pre-roll spend; heaviest volume buyers. Core segment for all stores.
🧓50+ consumers: FASTEST GROWING trial segment. Motivated by sleep, pain, anxiety. Less price-sensitive. More per-visit spend.
👩Women: 1 in 3 now consume; prefer edibles and infused products. Greenfield + suburban CA stores should curate for this.
🎓Gen Z (1997–2012): 18.5% of pre-roll spend, growing. Strong SDSU / UMass demographic opportunity.
🎖️Veterans: Well-defined discount-responsive niche. La Mesa + Lake Elsinore (military adjacent) = priority for vet programs.

📣 Marketing Channel Performance

🗺️Weedmaps: Primary discovery platform. Algorithm favors complete profiles, photos, fresh menus, reviews, geo-keywords.
🔍Google Business Profile: Critical for "dispensary near me" searches. Accuracy = ranking factor.
💬SMS/Email Marketing: Highest ROI channel for cannabis (Springbig). Compliant, direct, conversion-driven.
🛒Online Ordering: 25% of all sales now online. Dutchie menu embed + pickup orders = conversion opportunity.
🎁Loyalty Programs: Single highest-impact retention tool. Priority for Fyre Ants + Greenfield customer re-activation.
