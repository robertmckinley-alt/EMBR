Competitive Threat Assessment — Easthampton Market (May 2026)
🔴 OZ CLUB (OUNCE CLUB) — 17 East St, Easthampton CRITICAL THREAT
Opened September 2025. "State's first true cannabis outlet dispensary." Positioning: lowest prices in Massachusetts via outlet/bulk model. Same ownership as Honey Northampton and Dreamer (Wemelco Industries). Hours: 9AM-9PM 7 days. Targets I-91 commuters — "2 minutes from I-91." Products: full menu (flower, pre-rolls, edibles, concentrates, vapes). Large, warehouse-style inventory on open display shelves. Explicitly challenges ALL Easthampton incumbents on price.

Response required: Fyre Ants must compete on service quality, product curation, loyalty, and community identity — NOT on price matching. Pre-roll + curated flower differentiation critical.
🟡 INSA — 122 Pleasant St (Keystone Mill), Easthampton HIGH THREAT
First permitted dispensary in Easthampton. 100,000 sq ft cultivation facility = vertically integrated. Direct supply chain = structural cost advantage over Fyre Ants. Full product menu. Established customer base. Strong brand recognition across MA.

Note: INSA is now also threatened by OZ Club on price — may shift upmarket, which could open mid-tier space for Fyre Ants.
🔵 Dreamer — Southampton (adjacent town) MODERATE
Same Wemelco ownership as OZ Club and Honey. In Southampton, not Easthampton city limits but competes in trade area. Positioned as distinct character from OZ Club and Honey per owners' stated strategy.
